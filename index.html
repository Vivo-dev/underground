01

No Slides. Only Prototypes.

Focused companies beat unfocused ones. We back founders who know exactly what they're solving, and why they're the ones to solve it.

Deep tech doesn't exit in 18 months. We're patient, hands-on, and here for the full journey — from lab to market to category leadership.
