The Manifesto

"We are a generation of men raised by machines. We're the middle children of history, man. No purpose or place. We have no Great War. No Great Depression. Our great war is a spiritual war. Our great depression is our lives."

But we found our war. In the circuits. In the steel. In the code that will reshape reality itself. We are the underground of deep tech, funding the rebels who build the future in garages and labs, away from the noise of the market.
